Navigating Your Network is a blog post I wrote for the Wily Network a Boston based program that seeks to empower students with the necessary tools to succeed in college and beyond. In this article I talk about my own struggles with networking, and offer tips on building and maintaining relationships with mentors. My goal is to hopefully help other students with non-traditional backgrounds as they face their own unique challenges navigating their lives.
